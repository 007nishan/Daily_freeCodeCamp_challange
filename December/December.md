<h1>Purge Most Frequent</h1>
Given an array of values, remove all occurrences of the most frequently occurring element and return the resulting array.
<ul>
<li>If multiple values are tied for most frequent, remove all of them.</li>
<li>Do not change any of the other elements or their order.</li>
</ul>
